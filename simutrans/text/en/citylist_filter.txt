<title>City List Help</title>

<h1><strong>City List</strong></h1>

<p>
The <strong>City list</strong> shows details about <a href="citywindow.txt">urban areas</a> (villages, towns and cities) and their populations.
</p>
<p>
To open: click the city list icon in the <a href="list.txt">list management</a> toolstrip, or press <a href="keys.txt">[T]</a>.<br>
<strong>City List</strong> has two option buttons which determine the order in which urban areas (and their populations) are shown.<br>
<em>Tip:</em> If items in list are only partially visible, then <a href="mouse.txt">re-size</a> the <strong>City List</strong> window or scroll the list using the slider bar.
</p>
<p>
<em>Population:</em> is the total number of citizens in the game world (the total population of all urban areas). Any recent increase in population is shown in brackets.
</p>
<p>
<em>Sorted by:</em> has options to determine the order of urban areas shown in list.<br>
Click the option buttons to cycle through options (changes name of the option button):
</p>
<p>
- <em>Name</em> lists the cities in alphabetical order of name.<br>
- <em>Population</em> lists the cities in numerical order by size of their populations.<br>
- <em>City growth</em> olists the cities by their growth rate.
</p>
<p>
- <em>The up arrow</em> and <em>the down arrow</em> determine whether the list is in ascending or descending order.
</p>
<p>
Click on the name of a town or city to see more <a href="citywindow.txt">information</a> about that urban area.<br>
Items listed for each urban area include:
</p>
<p>
- name;
</p>
<p>
- population; and
</p>
<p>
- any recent increase or decrease in population (shown by the upward pointing blue or downward pointing red arrow respectively).
</p>
